Welcome to the MIT App Inventor 2 Preview

To Get Started, click New Project in the
upper left. You will have a working app
in no time if you follow these
Quick Introductory Tutorials.
New System Deployed November 11, 2013. You will need a new companion
You can obtain here.
Design your app in the Design Window
and program it in the Blocks Editor.
Test it by Connecting your Android device.

No Android Device? No Problem!
Use the built-in, on-screen Android emulator.
(Emulator and USB connections are currently for Mac only. Support for Windows and Linux is coming soon!)
