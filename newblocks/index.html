Welcome to App Inventor "newblocks" pilot

Welcome to the pre-Alpha test of the next generation of App Inventor. The most significant change in this version is the moving of the blocks editor into the browser instead of residing in a separate Java program.

A lot about this new version is very very cool. A lot about this version is very very unstable! Even the exact name that we will call it is not fully decided. Colloquially we have referred to this version as "newblocks."

Keep in mind that this is very much a work in progress. We believe this version works well enough to give you a feel for where we are going, and we welcome feedback. However it is NOT SUITABLE FOR PRODUCTION OR CLASS USE.

It is also undergoing continuous update and improvement. Do not be surprised if it looks different from day to day. We know that not all of it works (though do let us know when you find something broken). We may also break it (accidentally) from time to time.

Access to this version is by invitation only. Chances are if you are reading this note, you have been invited and have been added to our list of allowed users. But keep in mind that not everyone can use this version (yet).

Finally. Thank You for volunteering to help us improve App Inventor! We very much appreciate your help and feedback!

You will need the new MIT NewBlocks Companion App

You will need to download the MITNewBlocksCompanion.apk to do incremental development. This app is not available in the Google Play Store. You will need to download it here and side load it into your device. Only wireless connectivity is currently supported. We also do not yet have an emulator version, but we are planning on having one.

The Newblocks Companion can (and does) co-exist with the MIT AICompanion App, so you can have both on your device without difficulty.
