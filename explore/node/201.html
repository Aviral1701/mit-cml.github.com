2013 App Inventor Summit @ MIT

Wednesday, July 17 & Thursday, July 18, 2013
Massachusetts Institute of Technology,  Cambridge, MA

The MIT Center for Mobile Learning will host a two-day summit for the App Inventor community. This conference will allow time for small working groups to develop plans for collaboration around App Inventor. To ensure a balanced mix of participants registration is by invitation only. We will do our best to accommodate all who would like to attend, but will give first priority to people who are working currently working with App Inventor. Deadline Extended Apply by May 31.

Invited presenters will share their work. Proposals for talks and posters* are due by May 18th.
*Students are welcome to apply to take part in the poster session on Thursday. Fill out the proposal form and indicate the estimated number of students you would like to bring with you. Students do not have to apply to attend the summit. The proposal form serves as their application.

General information

The Summit will take place in the Stata Center at MIT in the Kendall Square area of Cambridge.
It will at 8:30am on both days and end by 4 p.m. on June 18th.
Breakfast and Lunch will be provided both days.
Participants are responsible for their own transportation and travel costs.
Information on overnight accommodations is below - please make your arrangements early!
Check out general MIT Visitor Information and area hotels

Note to CSTA Annual Conference attendees

Want to make a week of your CSTA experience? The MIT Center for Mobile Learning and Scheller Teacher Education Program are offering 3 great days of programming on July 17, 18, and 19. On July 17 and 18, join MIT CML for the 2013 App Inventor Summit. Then, on July 19, attend the Introduction to Programming with Imagaination Toolbox workshop and learn how to create games and simulations that run in a web browser using the new online version of StarLogo TNG!
