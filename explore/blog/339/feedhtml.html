Submit your app to be our 'App of the Month'! </h3> <p></p> <p>The MIT App Inventor team is looking for innovative apps built using App Inventor for our <b>App of the Month Program</b>! Selected apps will be featured on our webpage. We are considering apps in four categories:</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Apps for Communities (i.e., apps that help organizations, companies, governments)</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Apps for Individuals (i.e., apps that help individuals)</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Apps for Research (i.e., apps that support research)</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Apps for Education (i.e., apps that support education)</p> <p><br /></p> <p>If you’d like to submit an app you created in App Inventor, please email the <a href="mailto:aiwebreview@mit.edu?subject=Application for App of the Month">MIT App Inventor Team</a> with the following information: </p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Your App’s title</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Which category(ies) you’re submitting it for?</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• What the app does?</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• Why did you build it?</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• What is your name, age, profession?</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• What is the current status of the app? (e.g., Is it on the Play Store?, Has it won awards?)</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• 1-3 screenshots of the app</p> <p style="text-indent: 20px">• App’s .apk file OR a link to the app on the play store, if applicable</p> </div></div></div><section class="field field-name-field-genre field-type-list-text field-label-above view-mode-rss"><h2 class="field-label">Genre:&nbsp;</h2><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even">Blog</div></div></section> Mon, 19 May 2014 16:48:04 +0000 ruff 513 at http://explore.appinventor.mit.edu
